# Code of Conduct

We run on a simple standard: **be good to people.**

The golden rule — treat others the way you treat yourself — doesn't go far enough,
because plenty of people treat themselves like shit. So the bar here is higher.
Call it the righteous rule: treat others *well*, regardless of how you treat
yourself. Assume good faith, hold the door, leave people better than you found
them.

In practice:

- Participation here is harassment-free. Full stop.
- Be respectful of people you disagree with. Go after the work, not the person.
- Give and take feedback in good faith.

Behavior that doesn't clear that bar — harassment, personal attacks, posting
people's private information, that kind of thing — isn't welcome here, and the
maintainers will act on it.

If something crosses the line, report it privately to the maintainers through the
repository's **Security** tab (private reporting) or the project's published
contact. Reports are handled discreetly.
